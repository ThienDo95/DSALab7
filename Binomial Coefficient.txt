Binomial Coefficient
	_ Andreas von Ettingshausen [ German mathematician & physicist ] introduced binomial coeffient  in 1826
		. Notation (n)
			   (k)
	_ Refer to specific address in Pascal's Triangle
		. They refer nth row, rth element in Pascal's triangle
		. For example :	(5)
			        (4)
		. 5th row -- 4th element
